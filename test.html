Key Formulas
Speed (S): S = Distance / Time
Time (T): T = Distance / Speed
Distance (D): D = Speed × Time
Downstream (Sd): Sd = Boat speed + Stream speed
Upstream (Su): Su = Boat speed − Stream speed
Boat speed (still): = (Sd + Su) ÷ 2
Stream speed: = (Sd − Su) ÷ 2
Q1 • Time formula Basic
A train covers 120 km at a constant speed of 40 km/h. What is the time taken?
Time = Distance ÷ Speed = 120 ÷ 40 = 3 hours.
Q2 • Speed formula Concept
Which of the following correctly gives speed when distance (D) and time (T) are known?
By definition, Speed = Distance ÷ Time.
Q3 • Distance formula Easy
A bus runs at 60 km/h for 2.5 hours. What distance does it cover?
Distance = Speed × Time = 60 × 2.5 = 150 km.
Q4 • Downstream speed Boat & Stream
A boat can move at 12 km/h in still water. The speed of the stream is 3 km/h. What is the downstream speed?
Downstream speed = Boat speed + Stream speed = 12 + 3 = 15 km/h.
Q5 • Upstream speed Boat & Stream
A boat moves at 18 km/h in still water and the stream flows at 4 km/h. What is the upstream speed?
Upstream speed = Boat speed − Stream speed = 18 − 4 = 14 km/h.
Q6 • Boat speed formula Concept
Downstream speed of a boat is 20 km/h and upstream speed is 12 km/h. What is the speed of the boat in still water?
Boat speed in still water = (Downstream + Upstream) ÷ 2 = (20 + 12) ÷ 2 = 16 km/h.
Q7 • Stream speed formula Concept
If the downstream speed is 15 km/h and upstream speed is 9 km/h, what is the speed of the stream?
Stream speed = (Downstream − Upstream) ÷ 2 = (15 − 9) ÷ 2 = 3 km/h.
Q8 • Application Speed & Distance
A man walks 5 km at 4 km/h and then 3 km at 6 km/h. What is his total time taken?
Time = D1/S1 + D2/S2 = 5/4 + 3/6 hours.
Q9 • Boat & stream time Application
A boat goes 24 km downstream in 2 hours. The stream speed is 2 km/h. What is the speed of the boat in still water?
Downstream speed = 24 ÷ 2 = 12 km/h. Boat speed = 12 − 2 = 10 km/h.
Q10 • Speed comparison Banking style
A train increases its speed from 60 km/h to 80 km/h over the same distance. How will the time change?
For same distance, Time ∝ 1/Speed. So ratio of times = 1/60 : 1/80 = 80 : 60 = 4 : 3. Hence time decreases in the ratio 4 : 3.
